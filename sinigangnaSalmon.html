Sinigang na Salmon

Ingredients


  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 2 pcs onions, quartered
  • 4 pcs native tomatoes, quartered
  • ½ kg salmon, cut into 1 to 1½-inch-thick pcs
  • 4 cups rice washing
  • 4-6 pcs okra, sliced
  • 2 pcs long green chili
  • 1 (22 g) pack Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix na may Miso
  • ½ bunch kangkong, cut into 1-inch lengths

How to Cook Sinigang na Salmon:
1. Start by sauteing onions and tomatoes in hot oil
until the tomatoes are very soft. Add salmon pieces
and gently sauté. Pour in rice washing and let it boil gently.
2. Next, throw in the okra and chili. Pour in the
sinigang mix before adding kangkong. Stir and simmer.
It should be done in a minute. Season according to preference.
3. Serve hot with rice, patis, calamansi, and chili.
