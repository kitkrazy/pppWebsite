Pinaupong Manok

Ingredients

  • 1 k Chicken Whole
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 2 pcs Onion quartered
  • 1 head Garlic crushed
  • 1 pc Ginger sliced
  • lemongrass
  • ½ tsp msg - optional
  • 3 cups Salt
  • 1 pc Banana Leaf or Foil cut to the diameter of the pot
  • 2 liter Water
  • 3 tbsp Salt

How To Cook Pinaupong Manok:
1. Dissolve 3 tbsp salt in water and submerge chicken for at
least 30 minutes(ideally 12hrs).
  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 2 liter Water
    • 2 liter Water
  • 3 tbsp Salt

2. Level 3 cups salt or about 2 inches deep in the bottom of the
pot and place banana leaf or foil on top.
  • 3 cups Salt
  • 1 pc Banana Leaf or Foil

3. Drain and dry chicken with a paper towel and season chicken with
mixed salt, pepper and msg(optional) inside and outside
massaging to the entire chicken skin.
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Ground Black Pepper
  • ½ tsp msg

4. Stuff chicken with lemongrass, onions, garlic and ginger.
  • 2 pcs Onion
  • 1 head Garlic
  • 1 pc Ginger
  • 1 bunch lemon grass

5. Tie chicken's legs together to secure stuffing.
6. Sit chicken on top of banana leaf/foil and cover with tight-fitting pot cover.
7. Cook on medium heat for 50 mins or until the chicken is tender.
8. Serve with soy sauce and lemon/calamansi mixture dipping sauce.
