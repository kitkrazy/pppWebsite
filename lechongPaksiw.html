Lechon Paksiw

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Lechon Pork Belly, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Mang Tomas all purpose sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/3 tbsp whole peppercorn
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • salt and ground pepper to taste

How to Cook Lechon Paksiw:
1. Arrange lechon meat in the pot.
2. Top lechon meat with onions, garlic, peppercorns and bay leaves.
3. Combine vinegar, soy sauce, water and sugar in a bowl.
Stir well to combine. Taste test to see if more salt, pepper or
sugar is needed.
4. Pour soy sauce mixture over the meat and cover the pot.
5. Turn on Healthy Multi Pot and press on the Meat Stew button.
That will cook the lechon paksiw for 30 minutes. The pot will automatically
turn off once your dish is ready. Allow the steam to come out
before opening the pot.
6. Transfer in a serving dish and serve with steam rice. Enjoy!
