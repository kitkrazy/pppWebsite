Chicken Tinola

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo whole chicken, cut into pieces.
  • 1 small young unripe papaya or chayote, cut into small pieces.
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, crushed and sliced into strips
  • 1/2 cup dahon ng sili (chili leaves) or malunggay leaves
    (spinach can also be used)
  • 1 liter of water
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 4 tablespoons
  • 2 tablespoons patis (fish sauce), or use salt instead
  • A small portion of lemon grass (small knot)

How to Cook Tinolang Manok
1. In a stock pot, heat oil and saut√© garlic, onion and ginger.
2. Add water and the chicken.
3. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 20 minutes or
until the chicken is almost done.
4. Season with patis or salt.
5. Add papaya and lemon grass.
Simmer for an additional 5 minutes or
until papaya softens but not overcooked.
6. Add the chili leaves and malunggay leaves then turn off the heat.
7. Serve steaming hot on a bowl with plain rice on the side.
