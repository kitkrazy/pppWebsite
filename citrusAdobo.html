Citrus Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1/8 bar butter
  • 3/4 bulb garlic, minced
  • 600 g pork, sliced into cubes
  • 1/4 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1/8 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 1/2 tbsps Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 4 pcs calamansi
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp whole peppercorn
  • 1 1/2 tbsps white sugar
  • 2 pcs bay leaves
  • 2 pcs egg, boiled and peeled
  • 8 pcs quail eggs, boiled and peeled

    • For the appetizer:
  • 1/8 tsp ginger, chopped
  • 2 pcs big ripe potatoes,chopped
  • 1/4 tsp fish sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Knorr Liquid Seasoning

How to Cook Citrus Adobo:
1. Get your pan nice and hot over medium heat. Melt the butter
and throw in the garlic. Add the pork and continue saut√©ing until
the pork is slightly brown.
2. Pour in the oyster sauce, pineapple juice, and Knorr
Liquid Seasoning. Let mixture simmer before adding calamansi
extract, soy sauce, water, and peppercorns and allow it to boil.
3. If the pork is already tender, add the white sugar, bay leaves
and boiled eggs. Allow this to simmer one last
time and turn heat off.
4. For the appetizer: Slice ginger and tomatoes into
strips then mix in a bowl. Add fish sauce and Knorr Liquid Seasoning,
and toss together.
5. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with hot steam rice and enjoy!
