Bistek-Tagalog/BeefSteak

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs Beef, sliced thin
  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Lemon, juiced
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • Fresh ground pepper, to taste
  • 1 Large Onion, cut into rings
  • 1 tbsp Avocado oil
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup Green onion, sliced

How to Cook Bistek-Tagalog Beefsteak:
1. In a large zip lock bag, combine the sliced beef,
garlic, juice of 1 lemon, soy sauce, brown sugar and,
fresh ground pepper and knead in the bag to ensure
that all the marinade is coating the beef.
2. Set this aside in the fridge for at least 8 hours.
I let mine sit in the marinade overnight and it was so flavorful!
3. Cut the onion into rings.
4. In a stainless steel sauté pan set to medium heat
add avocado oil and add the onions. Cook the onions
until it’s soft. Set the onions aside.
5. Add more oil into the pan and start frying up the
beef making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
6. Fry the beef on both sides and set it aside on
a large platter.
7. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water
and set it aside.
8. Add the remaining marinade in the pan and add
the cornstarch mixture.
9. Let this come to a boil, then bring it down to
a low simmer for about 5 minutes.
10. Pour the sauce all over the beef and top
with the onions.
11. If you’d like, you can add some green onions
on top as garnish.
12. Serve with white rice.
