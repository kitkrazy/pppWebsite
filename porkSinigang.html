Pork Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 900g of pork spare ribs, cut into 5cm long pieces
  • 1.9l of water
  • 2 tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 1 onion, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 30ml of fish sauce
  • 6 gabis, peeled and cut into halves or quarters depending on their size
  • 2 finger chillies
  • 1 radish, peeled, and cut into 2cm thick slices
  • ½ a bunch of long beans, cut into 7cm long pieces with their ends trimmed
  • 1 eggplant, cut into 2cm thick slices with their ends trimmed
  • 6 okras, ends trimmed
  • 60g of tamarind base powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 bunch of bok choy, separated into leaves

How to Cook Sinigang na Baboy:
1. Rinse the pork ribs with water and drain well.
2. Add the pork ribs and 1.9l of water into a large pot and bring to a boil.
Skim any scum that accumulates on top.
3. Add the tomatoes, onion, and fish sauce. Reduce the heat and
let it simmer for 1 to 1 ½ hours.
4. Add the gabi’s and let it cook for 4 to 6 minutes.
5. Add the finger chillies and radish. Simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
6. Add the long beans and let them cook for 2 minutes.
7. Then, add the eggplant and okra and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
8. Add the tamarind base powder and stir well until it dissolves.
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
10. Lastly, add the boy choy and let it cook for 1 minute.
11. Transfer the pork sinigang to a large serving bowl.
Enjoy this as a soup or serve this with some hot steaming rice.
