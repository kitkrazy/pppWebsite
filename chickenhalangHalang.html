Chicken Halang Halang

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 thumb ginger sliced and crushed
  • 1 medium yellow onion chopped finely
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 pieces long green chilis chopped
  • 2 pounds chicken thigh, legs, and wings, cut serving pieces
  • 4 tablesoons fish sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon coarse ground pepper
  • 1 stalk lemongrass or ‘tanglad’ white part and green separated
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 small green papaya cut into cubes
  • 1 can coconut milk 400ml
  • 2 pieces chili labuyo uncut
  • Salt and pepper as needed
  • bunch chili leaves or malunggay (2 cups)

How to Cook Chicken Halang-Halang:
1. In a soup pot, sauté ginger strips in cooking oil.
2. Once brown, add in the onion and the garlic and cook
until lightly brown and softened.
3. Add in the chicken parts and sear all sides.
4. Add in the papaya, the lemon grass, water and fish sauce.
allow to boil and lower to a simmer.
5. Continue simmering until papaya and chicken are fully cooked.
6. Once the chicken parts are cooked, lower the heat and add in the
Alaska classic evaporated milk. mix well.
7. Add in the cornstarch slurry, malunggay leaves and the siling labuyo.
8. Add salt and pepper as needed.
9. Transfer to a serving plate and serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
