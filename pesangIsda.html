Pesang Isda

Ingredients


  • 10g Mama Sita’s Pang-Gisa Mix
  • 1 pc Onion, quartered
  • 2 slices Ginger, pounded
  • 120 g Green papaya, green, cut into serving pieces
  • 3 cups Water (or hugas Bigas)
  • 500 g Fish (Lapu-Lapu/grouper or Maya-Maya/snapper or Bacoco),
    cleaned and cut into serving pieces
  • 100g Cabbage
  • 5 pcs Pechay

How to Cook Pesang Isda:
1. In a saucepan, combine onion, ginger, Mama Sita’s
Pang-Gisa Mix, rice water and papaya. Boil for 5 minutes.
2. Add the fish and the cabbage, cook for another
5 to 7 minutes or until the fish is ready.
3. Add the pechay and cook for another 3 minutes.
Serve with patis and calamansi or sautéed miso paste.
