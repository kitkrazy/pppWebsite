Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs bone-in chicken parts, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorn
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 6-8 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • salt to taste
  • 5-10 dried red chili peppers or 1 tsp dried chili flakes
    (depends on how spicy you want)

How to Cook Chicken Adobo:
1. In a wide bowl, combine vinegar, soy sauce, paprika and sugar.
Stir well to dissolve sugar and paprika then add chicken pieces, peppercorns
and bay leaves. Mix well to coat chicken pieces with the marinade and cover
bowl. Refrigerate for at least an hour or overnight.
2. Remove chicken from the marinade and drain.
Save the remaining marinade.
3. In a wok or a wide pan, heat and add cooking oil over medium heat.
4. Add chopped onion and garlic. Cook for a minute until limp and aromatic.
Add chili peppers and stir. Cook for a minute.
5. Add marinated chicken and cook while stirring gently every few minutes
until chicken is light brown for about 5 to 10 minutes.
6. Pour in remaining marinade to the wok. Stir gently and bring to a
quick boil for about 2 minutes. Taste to see if more salt or sugar is needed.
7. Cover wok and reduce heat to simmer for about 15-20 minutes or
until chicken is fully cooked and meat starts to fall-off the bones.
Stir gently every few minutes. Add water if needed.
8. Remove cover and continue to cook until sauce is almost dry and
chicken starts to render on its own oil while stirring gently.
9. Transfer to a serving plate and serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
