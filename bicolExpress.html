Bicol Express

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 900g of pork belly
  • 15 ml of canola oil
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 30g of fresh shrimp paste
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 237 ml of water
  • 8 Thai chili peppers, minced
  • 6 finger chillies, sliced
  • 237ml of coconut cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

How to Cook Bicol Express:
1. Peel and thinly slice the onion, then cut the pork belly
into bite-size cubes. Set aside.
2. Pour the oil into a large pan and heat it over medium heat.
3. Add the onion and garlic into the pan. Cook until softened.
4. Next, add the pork and cook until lightly browned.
5. Add the shrimp paste and continue cooking for 1-2 minutes.
6. Add coconut milk, water, and chili peppers.
7. Bring it to a simmer and let it cook for 35 minutes.
By then, the pork should be tender, and the liquid
should be reduced, rendering fat.
8. Next, add the finger chillies and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring regularly.
9. Then, add coconut cream and let it simmer until it thickens
and renders fat.
10. Lastly, season with salt and pepper to taste
