Pininyahang Manok

Ingredients

  • ½ k Chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic minced
  • 1 med Onion sliced
  • 1 cup Pineapple Chunks fresh or in can(small)
  • 1 cup Coconut Milk
  • 1 piece Chicken Cube/Boullion
  • 1 piece Carrot - fried wedged
  • ½ cup Bell Pepper cut into thick strips
  • ½ cup Green Peas - cooked frozen or canned

How to Cook Pininyahang Manok
1. Sauté garlic and onion.
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic
  • 1 med Onion

2. Add in the chicken and cook until the color turns light brown.
  • ½ k Chicken

3. Add in pineapple including its juice, coconut milk,
chicken cube and let it boil.
  • 1 cup Pineapple Chunks
  • 1 cup Coconut Milk
  • 1 piece Chicken Cube/Bouillon

4. Simmer until the chicken is tender (approx 20 mins).
5. Add in the carrots, potatoes, bell pepper,
green peas and simmer for 2 minutes.
  • 1 piece Carrot - fried
  • 1 piece Potato - fried
  • ½ cup Bell Pepper
  • ½ cup Green Peas - cooked

6. Transfer to a serving dish and serve with rice.
