Beef Salpicao

Ingredients

  • ½ kilo ribeye beef (cut into cubes)
  • 4 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ½ teaspoon paprika powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 bunch Spring onions (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon toasted garlic

How to Cook Beef Salpicao:
1. Marinade beef tenderloin with garlic, olive oil, worcestershire
sauce, knorr seasoning and black pepper for at least 1 hour.
Overnight is an option too.
2. In a pan, stir fry garlic in olive oil until golden brown.
Set aside. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
3. Remove excess liquid from the marinade. Do not include
excess marinade so your salpicao won't be watery.
4. Use the same pan and heat olive oil until hot enough
for stir-fry. Make sure to use high heat.
5. Saute Beef for 3-4 minutes or until meat is brown.
Do not overcook.
6. Transfer to a serving plate.
7. Serve with Sinangag and Fried Eggs!
